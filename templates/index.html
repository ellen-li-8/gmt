SCOTUS Cert Scraper

Scrapes supremecourt.gov + Oyez · 2011–present · Exports to Excel

What this does: Fetches every Order List PDF from supremecourt.gov for terms 2011–2025, extracts all cases where certiorari was granted, then enriches each case with outcome and issue area data from the Oyez API. The bucket columns (Circuit Split, Federalism, etc.) are left for manual categorization — export to Excel and fill those in. Scraping all ~14 terms takes 3–7 minutes.
